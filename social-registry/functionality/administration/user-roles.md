# User Roles



<table data-header-hidden><thead><tr><th width="249">User Role</th><th>Details</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><strong>User Role</strong></td><td><strong>Details</strong></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Super Administrator</strong></td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: The highest level of administrative access.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Full control over the system, including all configuration settings.</li><li>Can manage other administrators and users.</li><li>Has access to all data and functionalities within the system.</li></ul></td></tr><tr><td><strong>System Administrator</strong></td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: Manages the technical and operational aspects of the system.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Ensures the system is running smoothly.</li><li>Manages system updates and maintenance.</li><li>Handles technical issues and user support.</li><li>Creates users and assigns and revokes privileges</li><li>May have some overlapping responsibilities with the Super Administrator but typically focuses on the technical side.</li></ul></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Crypto Administrator</strong></td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: Plays a crucial role within the security module of the system.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Manages and secures cryptographic keys.</li><li>Oversees encryption practices to ensure data security.</li><li>Ensures data integrity and protection against unauthorised access.</li><li>Maintains the confidentiality, availability, and integrity of sensitive information.</li><li>Can decrypt and view individual encrypted records or PII information</li><li>Normally this role is attached with other important roles</li></ul></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Registrar</strong></td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: Manages the registration process of individuals in the social registry.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Oversees the enrollment of new individuals.</li><li>Ensures that registration data is accurate and complete.</li><li>May validate and approve registration applications.</li></ul></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Data Administrator</strong></td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: Manages the data within the social registry.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Ensures data integrity and security.</li><li>Performs data updates and maintenance.</li><li>Manages data import and export processes.</li><li>Can create and manage data backups.</li></ul></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Data Validator</strong></td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: Ensures the accuracy and validity of the data collected.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Reviews and verifies data entries for accuracy and completeness.</li><li>Identifies and corrects errors or inconsistencies in the data.</li><li>Works closely with Data Administrators to maintain data quality.</li><li>Raises data correction requirements to data collectors or data administrators</li></ul></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Data Enumerator</strong><br>(Same as Data collector but offline)</td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: Collects data offline, often in the field.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Conducts surveys and gathers data from individuals or households.</li><li>Typically uses ODK or Kobo </li><li>Records data manually or using offline tools.</li><li>Submits collected data to the Data Administrator for integration into the system.</li></ul></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Data Collector</strong> (Registration portal user)</td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: Collects data through the registration portal.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Uses the online registration portal to collect and input data.</li><li>Ensures data is accurately entered into the system.</li><li>May assist individuals with the registration process through the portal.</li></ul></td></tr><tr><td><strong>Data Collection Supervisor</strong></td><td><p><strong>Role</strong>: Oversees and manages the data collection team within the registration portal to ensure accurate and efficient data gathering.</p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Supervises Data Collectors using the registration portal.</li><li>Coordinates data collection activities within the portal to ensure efficiency.</li><li>Monitors the quality of data being entered into the registration portal.</li></ul><p><br></p></td></tr></tbody></table>
